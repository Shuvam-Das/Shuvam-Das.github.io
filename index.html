About Me

An experienced Performance Test Engineer, I specialize in performance testing, monitoring, and defect resolution. My career journey has been marked by a strong passion for optimizing software applications to ensure peak performance and reliability. I thrive in environments that challenge me to enhance efficiency and contribute to impactful projects.

Currently, at Tech Mahindra, I collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to integrate performance enhancements seamlessly into existing systems. I am adept at adjusting testing strategies to meet changing project requirements, optimizing code quality, and creating custom benchmark tests to exceed client expectations. My proactive approach has significantly reduced production issues and increased the efficiency of test cycles through the development of reusable test scripts.

With a solid technical foundation in tools such as Dynatrace, Neoload, and JMeter, and proficiency in languages like Python and SQL, I bring a comprehensive skill set to any project. I have a proven track record of improving application performance and receiving positive stakeholder feedback for my contributions.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering and have earned multiple certifications, including the Business Analyst Certificate from CoachX and Microsoft Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900).

Beyond work, I stay updated with the latest technological advancements and enjoy listening to music and surfing the internet. Fluent in Bangla, English, and Hindi, I am equipped to engage and collaborate in diverse environments.
